About us What Are The Quotes All About?

Have you ever feel like a plastic bag, blowing in the wind? Do you ever feel like you in a very deep hole? And feel like no one see or hear your pain... well this is for you. Quotes are for the ones hurting. I know youre hurting. The quotes are for the uninspired. Because you dont have to feel like a waste in space, youre oringinal cant be replace.

Depression is like one big, black, hole. After it pulls you in, you cant get out. So, you spent countless nights crying yourself to sleep. You spend days in your room. You feel like doing absolutly nothing. You constantly hoping things will get better, but you're also wondering if it ever will. And the worst part? When you start to get happy again, depression pulls you back in. You crash harder than before.
